Building Creativity

Creativity

noun: "A phenomenon whereby something new and in some way valuable is created" Wikipedia

Research: Measuring Creativity

  • Creativity Quotient (CQ) - unsuccessful
  • Divergence Testing - find all possible answers instead of converging on the right one
  • Psychometric Study - tests to measure creativity such as unusual uses and uncommon responses to words

Torrance Tests of Creative Thinking

  • Fluency - The total number of relevant ideas generated in response to a stimulus
  • Originality - The statistical rarity of the responses
  • Elaboration - The amount of detail in the responses

Source: Wikipedia

Building Creativity

  • Have a purpose or intent
  • Reward curiosity and exploration
  • Provide opportunity for choice and discovery
  • Encourage confidence in risk-taking
  • Redefine problems (and solutions)
  • Start with many ideas and expand on them

Hindering Creativity

  • "That won't work"
  • "We've never done it that way"
  • "That idea isn't relevant"
  • "We don't know how to do that"
  • "I don't understand your idea"
  • "Let's find a solution everyone agrees on first"
