Engineering Open House

Build your Creativity

Curriculum Display

  • Lists the curriculum for new students
  • Overview of the department
  • Show degree options
  • What professionals in field do

Limited Class Displays

  • Show an aspect of what the department does
  • Cannot include class work or project judged for another competition
  • Interactive and relate to the public

Freshman/Sophomore Displays

  • How underclassmen are involved in department
  • First 2 years within department curriculum
  • Less technical than other displays
  • Show how younger students relate to field

Open Class Displays

  • Class or individual project
  • Can have been entered elsewhere
  • Winner gets $1000 award
  • Includes written portion
  • Senior project classes (CIS 59x)

Graduate Student Displays

  • Highlight research by graduate students
  • Engaging to the general public
  • Highlight real-world applications of research

Technical Displays

  • Any Open Class or Limited Class display
  • Requires a 2 page abstract
  • Rough draft due Feb 27th
  • Problem-and-solution format

Judging Displays

  • Top 3 displays in each category will be recognized by CIS
  • Department will choose 1 display for special recognition
  • 1st place CIS displays will be judged for College of Engineering Awards
  • Winners recognized at Engineering Awards Banquet April 10th

Yellow Brick Award

  • Parade Float
    • 8w x 12l x 8h
    • Follow Open House theme
  • Skit
    • 2 minutes or less
    • Show spirit & enthusiasm
    • Explain how department relates to theme
