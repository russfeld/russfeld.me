Course Structure

  • 30-45 minutes lecture material
  • 30-45 minutes activity/lab time
  • 30 lectures (2 unexcused absences)
  • 8 blog articles (drop 1)
  • 8 individual assignments (drop 1)
  • 2 team projects
  • 0 exams
  • 0 quizzes

Grading

  • 70% Individual Work

    • 21% Blog Articles (3% each)
